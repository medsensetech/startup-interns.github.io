Our Story

We’re a young and talented group of entrepreneurs with a groundbreaking idea that we hope will contribute towards a better tomorrow. We aspire to increase the employability of international students by connecting them with startups.

At Startup Intern, we believe that the right understanding and mutualism between international students and startups could work out the best chemistry for both parties. Contact us today to set up a meeting with one of our co-founder to know more about our project.
